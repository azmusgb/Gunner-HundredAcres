Chapter 1

The Beginning of Gunner's Tale

In the heart of the Hundred Acre Wood, where the sunlight dances through the leaves and the river sings a gentle song, a wonderful rumor began to spread...

A new little friend was coming to join their adventures. The bees buzzed it from flower to flower, the wind whispered it through the trees, and soon everyone in the Wood knew: Gunner was on his way.

"Oh, how exciting!" said Pooh, nearly dropping his honey pot. "We must prepare the coziest welcome!"

And so began the preparations. Pooh organized extra honey pots (just in case), Piglet practiced being extra brave (for both of them), and Tigger perfected his most wonderful welcoming bounce.
