Our Themed Tables

Eight round tables seat about 60 guests. Every table shares the same base: white cloth, powder-blue runner, rattan charger, white plate, clear glassware, brushed-gold flatware, and a mini jar of local honey at each place setting. Colors change by character so the room feels cohesive, never chaotic.

The Story at Each Table

These cards are written for you, your florist, and your 3D-print brain. Each block spells out the figurine, florals, napkin color, and “feeling” so every table is distinct but clearly part of Gunner’s blue-and-honey world.

Pooh Bear Table

Pooh Bear

Honey Lover

3D-printed golden bear in a muted red shirt, standing beside a clear glass vase of white daisies, small yellow blooms, and soft greenery. Honey-yellow napkins echo the favors and keep the whole table glowing—a quiet nod to the idea that a day without friends is like an empty honey pot.

Palette: honey gold · soft white · fresh green.
Piglet Table

Piglet

Best Buddy

3D-printed tiny pink friend in a striped jumper, perched near a low compote of pale-pink roses, white blooms, and baby’s breath. Dusty-blush napkins add a gentle note that still feels boy-friendly against the powder-blue runner. The smallest friend with the biggest heart—for Gunner and everyone.

Palette: blush pink · ivory · powder blue.
Eeyore Table

Eeyore

Gentle Soul

3D-printed dusty-blue donkey with droopy ears, resting beside a tall vase of dusty-blue blooms, white daisies, and eucalyptus. Slate-blue napkins deepen the calm, layered over the runner so this table feels like a quiet exhale. It softly says, “You’re noticed, you’re wanted, you’re home.”

Palette: dusty blue · grey · white · eucalyptus green.
Tigger Table

Tigger

Bouncy Friend

3D-printed energetic tiger cub with bright stripes, leaning forward mid-bounce. The centerpiece mixes marigolds, vibrant yellow blooms, and pops of orange in a low vase. Marigold napkins bring motion and warmth without overpowering the room— perfect for game cards, big laughs, and “bouncy” photos with Gunner.

Palette: tangerine · marigold · warm neutral.
Kangaroo Table

Kangaroo

Motherly Love

3D-printed warm tan kangaroo with a baby joey nestled in her pouch, seated beside a vase of cream and peach florals with soft greenery. Warm beige or pale-peach napkins create a cozy, held-close feeling—perfect for parents, grandparents, and anyone who has carried somebody else through.

Palette: tan · peach · cream · soft green.
Rabbit Table

Rabbit

Gardener

3D-printed slender rabbit figurine in pale yellow, positioned near a garden-style arrangement of daisies, yellow accents, and fresh herbs. Pale-yellow napkins echo the blooms and keep the table feeling like a small spring garden—busy, but never too busy for friends.

Palette: butter yellow · bright white · herb green.
Owl Table

Owl

Wise Friend

3D-printed tan-and-cream owl with tiny round glasses, perched near an urn of cream florals and deep greenery. Sage-green napkins add an earthy, thoughtful layer. This is the best table for “wishes for baby” cards and quiet, wise conversation.

Palette: sage · cream · forest green.
Hero Table

Gunner’s Table

Guest of Honor

3D-printed stylized little boy figure in soft blue and white, standing at the center of a low ring of white-and-pale-blue blooms with light greenery. Light-blue napkins are just a shade lighter than the runner so this table reads as the hero of the room. Place near the entrance so guests see it the moment they walk in.

Palette: baby blue · white · soft greenery.
