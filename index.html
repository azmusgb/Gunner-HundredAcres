Our Themed Tables

Every round table seats 6–8 guests and shares the same base look: white cloth, powder-blue runner, rattan charger, white plate, clear glassware, brushed-gold flatware, and a mini jar of local honey at each setting. Colors shift by character so the room feels cohesive, never chaotic.

The Story at Each Table

These cards are written for you, your florist, and your 3D-print brain. Each block spells out the figurine, florals, napkin color, and the “feeling” we’re building so every table is distinct but still clearly part of Gunner’s blue-and-honey world.

Pooh Bear Table

Pooh Bear

Honey Lover

Golden bear in a muted red shirt with white daisies, small yellow blooms, and gentle greenery. Honey-yellow napkins echo the favors and keep the whole table glowing. A quiet nod to the idea that a day without friends is like an empty honey pot.

Palette: honey gold · soft white · fresh green.
Piglet Table

Piglet

Best Buddy

Tiny pink friend in a striped jumper with pale-pink roses, white flowers, and baby’s breath. Dusty-blush napkins add a soft, gentle note that still feels boy-friendly against the blue runner. The smallest friend with the biggest heart—for Gunner and everyone.

Palette: blush pink · ivory · powder blue.
Eeyore Table

Eeyore

Gentle Soul

Droopy-eared dusty-blue donkey with dusty-blue blooms, white daisies, and eucalyptus. Slate-blue napkins and the powder-blue runner keep the palette calm and comforting. It quietly says: “Thanks for noticing me— we absolutely do.”

Palette: dusty blue · grey · white · eucalyptus green.
Tigger Table

Tigger

Bouncy Friend

Energetic orange tiger cub with bright yellow-and-orange florals. Marigold napkins bring warmth and motion without overpowering the room. This is the table for big laughs, goofy games, and “bouncy” photos with Gunner.

Palette: tangerine · marigold · warm neutral.
Kangaroo Table

Kangaroo

Motherly Love

Warm tan kangaroo with a baby joey in her pouch, surrounded by cream-and-peach florals. Warm beige or pale-peach napkins keep things cozy and nurturing. Perfect for parents, grandparents, and anyone who has carried somebody else through.

Palette: tan · peach · cream · soft green.
Rabbit Table

Rabbit

Gardener

Slender pale-yellow rabbit with garden-style florals: daisies, yellow accents, and herbs. Pale-yellow napkins echo the flowers and keep the table feeling like a little spring garden—busy, but never too busy for friends.

Palette: butter yellow · bright white · herb green.
Owl Table

Owl

Wise Friend

Tan-and-cream owl with tiny round glasses, surrounded by cream florals and deep greenery. Sage-green napkins add an earthy, thoughtful touch. Great spot for advice cards, “wishes for baby,” and warm conversation.

Palette: sage · cream · forest green.
Hero Table

Gunner’s Table

Guest of Honor

Simple stylized little boy figure in soft blue and white, with white-and-pale-blue blooms and light greenery. Light-blue napkins are just a shade lighter than the runner so this table reads as the “hero” of the room.

Palette: baby blue · white · soft greenery.
